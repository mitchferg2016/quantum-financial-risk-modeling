# Quantum Financial Risk Modeling

## Independent Research Initiative

This repository explores advanced approaches to financial risk modeling, with a long-term focus on integrating emerging computational paradigms such as quantum computing.

---

## 📌 Overview

Traditional financial risk models rely heavily on:

* Historical statistical assumptions
* Linear or simplified relationships
* Monte Carlo simulations with classical constraints

While effective, these approaches struggle to capture:

* Complex, high-dimensional correlations
* Non-linear market dynamics
* Tail-risk behavior in extreme conditions

This project investigates how these limitations may be addressed through more advanced modeling techniques.

---

## 🎯 Research Direction

The initiative focuses on:

* Regime-aware market modeling
* Volatility clustering and tail-risk estimation
* Hybrid classical + emerging computational approaches
* Exploration of quantum-enhanced optimization and simulation

---

## 🧠 Relationship to Engineering Platform

This research is supported by an implementation platform:

👉 **stock-ml-platform** (separate repository)

This repository represents the **research layer**, while the platform represents the **execution layer**.
